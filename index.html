US President Prediction

Final Project for Data Analytics BootCamp 2019

Introduction

With the upcoming 2020 election only being a year away, we thought it would be interesting as well as entertaining to predict what the future holds for the United States. Because of the relative complexity of the Presidential election, there was a wide variety of data that could potentially be used. However, our team chose to look at past elections to predict those in the future as well as looking into more fluid factors like sentiment via Twitter.
